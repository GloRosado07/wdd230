Audience

The Guapiles Chamber of Commerce's target audience is local businesses located in Guapiles and sorrounded areas, whether it is a start-up or well-established business. The website will be a place where Guapiles residents can find out more about businesses near them, and a place where businesses can network and be highlighted.
